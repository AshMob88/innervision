Personal Data

Our nominated representative for the purpose of the Act is Ali Mobasser. Please contact ali@innervision.ie if you wish to discuss any matters relating to the processing of your personal data.
When you contact us via the form on our website we collect your name, email address and any other information supplied by you in relation to your query. We collect and process this information only for the purposes of dealing with your query and any appointment that may follow. Should you attend for an appointment at Innervision, we will gather further information about you and your pregnancy for the purposes of providing our services to the highest professional standards. The data you provide will only be processed for the purposes of your query and any appointment or follow up communication which is necessary for the provision of the services provided by Innervision.
We will not disclose your personal data to anyone who is not an employee of Innervision or is not involved in communication with you or providing services on behalf of Innervision. Notwithstanding this, your personal data may be disclosed by Innervision where this is required by law.

Should you wish to exercise these rights, please send a request in writing to the address on our contact page or email a href="mailto:info@innervision.ie">info@innervision.ie.  We will respond to your request within 40 days.
